Your privacy is our top priority

Cast IP TV Player is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on external servers. All data remains on your device and is never shared with third parties.

How We Use Your Information

All information stored by Cast IP TV Player is used exclusively for app functionality:

  • IPTV Credentials: Used to authenticate and connect to your IPTV service provider
  • Playlist Data: Cached locally to provide faster loading and offline access to channel lists
  • Viewing History: Stored to help you quickly resume watching and find recently viewed content
  • App Settings: Saved to maintain your preferences like language, background theme, and display options

Data Storage and Security

Your data security is paramount:

  • All data is stored locally on your Android TV device using encrypted storage
  • Credentials are protected using Android's secure storage mechanisms
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
  • Data is only accessible by the Cast IP TV Player app on your device
  • You can delete all stored data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

Third-Party Services

Cast IP TV Player connects to third-party IPTV services that you provide credentials for. Please note:

  • We do not control or have access to third-party IPTV services
  • Your IPTV provider may have their own privacy policies
  • Data exchanged with IPTV services is subject to their privacy practices
  • We recommend reviewing your IPTV provider's privacy policy

Permissions Required

Cast IP TV Player requires the following Android permissions:

  • Internet Access: To stream content from your IPTV service
  • Network State: To check connectivity and optimize streaming
  • Storage: To cache playlist data and store preferences locally

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and not for data collection.

Children's Privacy

Cast IP TV Player is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to us, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally and accessible through the app
  • Deletion: Clear app data or uninstall to remove all stored information
  • Modification: Update your credentials and preferences anytime in Settings
  • Export: Your playlist data can be exported through the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our Contact Us page. We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns promptly.
